Bio

Po-Nan Li is a second-year EE PhD student at Stanford, working with professors Soichi Wakatsuki and Piero Pianetta. His research interests include but not limited to X-ray coherent diffraction imaging, computational imaging and biological computation.

Prior to joining Stanford in 2015, Po-Nan was a research assistant at Academia Sinica, Taiwan. He received his master's degree in photonics and his bachelor's degree in EE from National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan, in 2012 and 2010, respectively.
